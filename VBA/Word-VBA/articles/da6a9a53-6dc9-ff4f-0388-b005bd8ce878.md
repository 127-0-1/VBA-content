
# Trendline.ClearFormats Method (Word)

Clears the formatting of the object.


## Syntax

 _expression_ . **ClearFormats**

 _expression_ A variable that represents a **[Trendline](1cfe897f-26ad-a838-ed9b-f3fd945ff7ea.md)** object.
