
# Point.ClearFormats Method (Word)

Clears the formatting of the object.


## Syntax

 _expression_ . **ClearFormats**

 _expression_ A variable that represents a **[Point](349ea9a3-9e9a-b26f-146f-799d39c3d4a9.md)** object.
