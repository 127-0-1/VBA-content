
# Series.ClearFormats Method (Word)

Clears the formatting of the object.


## Syntax

 _expression_ . **ClearFormats**

 _expression_ A variable that represents a **[Series](212c323f-8acb-2ba7-1359-ab0f43268e77.md)** object.
