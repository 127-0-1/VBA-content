
# LegendKey.ClearFormats Method (Word)

Clears the formatting of the object.


## Syntax

 _expression_ . **ClearFormats**

 _expression_ A variable that represents a **[LegendKey](07578528-3e73-7898-47dc-296aefb854f0.md)** object.
